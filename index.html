Engagement and Motivation in Learning: Emotion recognition can assess students' emotional states to help educators understand their engagement levels during classes. For instance, students may display signs of frustration or boredom when facing a challenging task, and emotion recognition can help address these issues in real-time. Kahoot!, an educational platform, uses emotion recognition to assess how students feel during lessons and adapt content to enhance engagement.
